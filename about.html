About Shreeyash Pratishthan


Shreeyash Technical Campus established under shreeyash pratishthan is situated in the midst of nature, surrounded by the hills, is Marathwada’s one of the most promising educational campus. It is spread over a land of 26.78 acres & it comprises of an Engineering College & MBA, a Polytechnic college,a D. Pharmacy & B. Pharmacy college. The campus was established in 2008 as a dream project of its chairman, Er. Basawaraj Mangrule, himself an engineer from Government College of Engineering, Aurangabad. Shreeyash Technical Campus has undertaken the responsibility of providing quality education to the students of Marathwada. Going beyond its motto of “Transforming Students into Professionals”, Shreeyash Technical Campus in a short span of time has played a significant role in bridging the gap between the demand and supply of the industrial zone of Aurangabad.
